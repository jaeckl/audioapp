# US-10-06: Capture without Play

## User story

As a **user**, I can **perform while transport is stopped** and commit notes with **Capture** (Ableton Note pattern).

## Acceptance criteria

- [x] Events recorded to capture buffer while armed
- [x] Capture button commits to new MIDI clip at playhead
- [x] Clear (✕) discards buffer
- [x] Works without pressing transport Play
