# US-10-01: Play tab workspace

## User story

As a **user**, I can open a **Play** tab that shows a fullscreen pad/keyboard surface (no timeline) so I can perform without cramped layout.

## Acceptance criteria

- [x] Play tab in bottom nav (icon only)
- [x] Play body hides arrangement and device strip
- [x] Compact transport remains visible
- [x] Context bar: track name, arm toggle, Pads|Keys switch
- [x] `enterPlayMode` starts audio output while transport stopped
